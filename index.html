About Cepstrum

Cepstrum is a student body of EEE department of IIT Guwahati. It consists of various modules like event, design, content, Webops, ARs, BRs. All are assigned their role and they work with proper coordination among all the team members. Currently, Priyanshu Jain is secretary of cepstrum. Thare are several initiatives taken by cepstrum like paperman, inplace, inphase, inplex, course review, freshers party, cofee house session, placement talks, intern Talks. Our Initiatives

Inplace

Inplace is the flagship publication of Cepstrum, IITG. This series features articles by junior and senior year students from the EEE department about their internship and placement experiences.

Inphase

Inphase is the departmental magazine of the Department of EEE, IIT Guwahati. Curated and published by the student body Cepstrum, it will also be a medium to gain valuable insights from the experiences of our alumni and from prominent people in the industry as well as academia.

Paperman

Not only the hero you need, but also the one you deserve! Paperman is one of its kind initiative by Cepstrum with the sole aim of creating a repository of past question papers and study resources to aid your academic needs.

Inked Intellects

This series features articles by sophomores of the EEE department, chronicling their experiences at various conferences and contests as well as their adventures in exploring new fields and domains.
